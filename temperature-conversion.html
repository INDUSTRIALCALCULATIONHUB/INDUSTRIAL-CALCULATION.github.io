About Temperature Conversion

This tool converts temperature values using standard thermodynamic relationships between Celsius, Fahrenheit, and Kelvin scales used in engineering calculations.

Applications

  • Thermodynamics
  • HVAC Systems
  • Process Engineering
  • Industrial Design Calculations

Theory

Temperature conversion is based on linear relationships between absolute and relative temperature scales used in SI and imperial systems.

Information

  • Celsius is based on water freezing/boiling points
  • Kelvin is absolute thermodynamic scale
  • Fahrenheit is commonly used in legacy systems

Notes

  • Always use Kelvin for thermodynamic equations
  • Negative Kelvin values are not physically possible
