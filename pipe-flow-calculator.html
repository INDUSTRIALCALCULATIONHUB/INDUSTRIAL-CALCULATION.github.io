About Pipe Flow Rate

Flow rate is the volume of fluid passing through a pipe per unit time using the continuity equation.

Formula

Q = A × V

  • Q = Flow rate
  • A = Cross-sectional area
  • V = Velocity

A = π × D² / 4 (D in meters)
