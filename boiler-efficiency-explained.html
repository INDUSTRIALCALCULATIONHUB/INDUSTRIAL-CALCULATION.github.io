Boiler Efficiency Explained

Understanding how industrial boilers convert fuel into useful energy

🔥 What is Boiler Efficiency?

Boiler efficiency is the ratio of useful heat energy output (steam) to the energy input from fuel. It shows how effectively a boiler converts fuel into usable thermal energy.

📐 Boiler Efficiency Formula

Efficiency (%) = (Heat Output / Heat Input) × 100

  • Heat Output = Energy in steam produced
  • Heat Input = Energy from fuel combustion

⚙️ Types of Boiler Losses

  • Flue gas loss
  • Radiation loss
  • Unburnt fuel loss
  • Blowdown loss

🏭 Factors Affecting Efficiency

  • Fuel quality
  • Excess air in combustion
  • Heat transfer surface condition
  • Operating pressure and load

📊 Industrial Applications

  • Power plants
  • Refineries
  • Chemical industries
  • Textile and food processing
