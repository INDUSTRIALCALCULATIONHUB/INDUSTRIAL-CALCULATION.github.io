Engineering Notes

This tool calculates Hopper Valley Angle and side plate angles based on geometric reduction from top to bottom opening. All values are in mm.

Formulas Used

R = √(((Lt-Lb)/2)² + ((Wt-Wb)/2)²)

Valley Angle = tan⁻¹(H / R)

Plate Angle (each side) = same symmetry assumption
