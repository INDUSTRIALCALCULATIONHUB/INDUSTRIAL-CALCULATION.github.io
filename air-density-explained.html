Air Density Explained

Engineering concept, formula and applications

What is Air Density?

Air density is the mass of air per unit volume. It is a key parameter in engineering calculations related to fluid flow, aerodynamics, and HVAC systems.

Formula

ρ = P / (R × T)

  • ρ = Air density (kg/m³)
  • P = Pressure (Pa)
  • T = Temperature (K)
  • R = Gas constant

Factors Affecting Air Density

  • Altitude (higher altitude → lower density)
  • Temperature (higher temperature → lower density)
  • Pressure

Applications

  • Aerodynamics (aircraft design)
  • HVAC calculations
  • Weather analysis
  • Industrial gas flow systems
