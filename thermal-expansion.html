Thermal Expansion Calculator

Calculate material expansion due to temperature change

About Thermal Expansion

Thermal expansion occurs when materials expand or contract due to temperature changes. It is critical in structural and mechanical design.

How This Calculator Works

The calculator uses the linear expansion formula ΔL = α × L × ΔT, where α is the coefficient of thermal expansion.

Applications

  • Piping system design
  • Bridge construction
  • Machine design
  • Rail track expansion

Limitations

This calculation assumes uniform material properties and does not include stress or deformation effects.
